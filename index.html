Reaching To Society(RTS), formally Reach To Homeless Society, is a club created in 2015 which focuses on volunteering for the society, so that people who are under-expressed can receive help. We go on a hiking trip and when we hike we turn on the app "Charity Miles" so that every mile we hike we raise money for a non-profit organization, specifically the National Multiple Sclerosis Society. Also every month, we go to San Francisco to serve food for homeless people in a soup kitchen/non-profit organization called "St. Anthony's". We go to “Fertile Groundworks” in Livermore to farm produce, which goes to local soup kitchens

Key Trips

  • Sunol Little Yosemite
  • Mission Peak
  • San Francisco Soup Kitchen
  • Mount Diablo
